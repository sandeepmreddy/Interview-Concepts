Choose ECS if:
You want to deploy Spring Boot/Dockerized apps quickly.

You prefer AWS-native solutions and don’t need full Kubernetes control.

You want to use Fargate (serverless containers) easily.

✅ Choose EKS if:

You want to use advanced K8s features (e.g., Helm, CRDs, service meshes).

You're managing large, complex, microservices architectures.
